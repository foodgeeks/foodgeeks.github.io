Garlic Shrimp

ryansnyder

Time

prep 0:15       total 0:15

Yield

4 servings

Ingredients

Ingredients

4 tbsp. olive oil
1/2 head garlic, minced
1 lb. shrimp, frozen, precooked, peeled and devined
1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper or
1/2 tsp. pepper
1-1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
1 tbsp. milk or
heavy cream
1 tbsp. butter
1 tbsp. sweetened lime juice
Rice or
pasta
Desired vegetables

Instructions

Preheat oil in medium-hot skillet. Throw in garlic. Let cook for 2 minutes. Add shrimp to skillet. Cover with pepper. Let cook for 5-6 minutes. Insert milk, cheese, butter and lime juice. Stir constantly for 5-6 minutes. Make sure cheese and butter are melted, and sauce is well mixed together. Serve with rice or pasta and steamed vegetables.
