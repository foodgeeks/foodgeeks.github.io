Ryan's Famous Pumpkin Nut Bread

ryansnyder

Time

prep 0:15       total 1:15

Yield

2 loaves

Ingredients

Ingredients

1 cup butter, melted
4 eggs
1 can (14-1/2 oz.) pumpkin
1/2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. ground cloves
1 tsp. ground nutmeg
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2-3/4 cup flour
1 cup chopped nuts

Instructions

Beat together eggs, butter, pumpkin and sugars; set aside. In a separate bowl, soft together flour, baking powder, baking soda and spices. Gradually add flour mixture to egg mixture; mix well. Add the chopped nuts and mix well. Spoon batter into 2 greased bread loaf pans. Bake for 1 hour at 350Â°F. Test with toothpick to ensure loaves are ready.

Author's Comments

A classic fall recipe.
