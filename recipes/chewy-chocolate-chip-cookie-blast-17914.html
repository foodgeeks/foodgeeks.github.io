Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookie Blast

michaelstone

Time

prep 0:20       total 0:40

Yield

12 to 15 servings

Ingredients

Ingredients

3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup softened butter or
margarine
1 egg
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla
1 dash salt
2-3/4 cup flour
6 oz. pecans, crushed
10 oz. milk chocolate chips

Instructions

Cream sugars with butter, preferably by fork. Add egg, soda, vanilla, and salt and stir well. Add flour and blend; dough will be quite dry. Stir in pecans and chocolate chips. Press tightly into patties approximately 3/4-inch thick and form round using thumb and pointer finger in circular mold. Place on greased cookie sheet or stone and bake at 375Â°F for 12-13 minutes. Don't overcook! The cookies will be soft inside and are best if made larger in size.

Author's Comments

Restaurant caliber cookies, best if made large.
