Butter Chicken

yursamall

Time

prep 0:15       total 0:30

Yield

6 servings

Ingredients

Ingredients

1 lb. chicken fillets, cubed
1 md. onion, sliced
4 tbsp. buttermilk
1 tsp. fresh ground ginger
1 tsp. fresh ground garlic
1 tsp. fine salt
2 tsp. fine red chilli powder
2 tsp. powdered cumin corriander blend
1/2 tsp. tumeric powder
1 can tomato paste (small)
1 oz. butter for sauteing
1 cup fresh cream
Chopped corrainder leaves for garnishing

Instructions

Mix buttermilk, ginger, garlic and spices together. Add tomato paste. Add washed and cubed chicken to marinade; allow to marinate for 30 minutes. While chicken is marinating, saute the onions in the butter, on medium heat. Add marinated chicken, cook for 15 minutes on medium-low heat. Remove from heat, add fresh cream and stir well. Return to heat, cook for 5 minutes. Garnish with chopped corriander leaves. Serve immediately.

Author's Comments

Varriation: The same recipe may be used for prawns!
