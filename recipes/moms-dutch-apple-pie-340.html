Mom's Dutch Apple Pie

emily

Time

prep 0:30       total 1:30

Yield

1 pies

Ingredients

Ingredients

Crust

1 cup flour
1/3 cup shortening
1/2 tsp. salt
3 tbsp. water

Pie

1/2 cup sugar
5 Jonathan apples, or
enough to fill crust
Cinnamon and nutmeg, to taste

Topping

1/2 cup sugar
3/4 cup flour
1/3 cup butter

Instructions

Crust:
Cut shortening into dry ingredients. Add water gradually until just moistened. Roll and place into a 9-inch pastry dish.

Filling:
Fill unbaked crust with peeled, sliced apples. Add 1/2 cup sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg to taste. Bake at 450°F for 15 minutes.

Take out of oven. Cut butter into flour and sugar for topping. Spoon over partially baked pie. Bake at 350°F degrees for an additional 45 minutes, or until the pie filling is bubbly.
