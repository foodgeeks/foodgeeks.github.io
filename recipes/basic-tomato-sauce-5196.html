Basic Tomato Sauce

kathleen_morrison

Time

Yield

6 cups

Ingredients

Ingredients

1 Spanish onion, cut into 1/4-inch dice
4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
3 fl. oz. virgin olive oil
4 tbsp. fresh thyme or
2 tbsp. dried thyme
1/2 md. carrot, finely shredded
2 cans (1-3/4 lb. each) crushed tomatoes, with their juices
Salt, to taste

Instructions

Saut√© the onion and garlic in the olive oil over medium heat until translucent but not brown (approximately 10 minutes). Add the thyme and carrot and cook 5 minutes more. Add the tomatoes. Bring to a boil, lower the heat to just bubbling, stirring occasionally for 30 minutes. Season with salt to taste. Serve immediately, or set aside for future use.

Author's Comments

The sauce may be refrigerated for up to one week or frozen for up to 6 months.
