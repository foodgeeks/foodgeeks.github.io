Devin's Banana Bread

ryansnyder

Time

prep 0:15       total 1:15

Yield

12 servings

Ingredients

Ingredients

1 stick margarine
2 eggs
4 md. ripe bananas
1 tsp. salt
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
2/3 cup sugar
1-3/4 cup flour
1 cup nuts

Instructions

Combine margarine, eggs and bananas. Add salt, baking powder, baking soda, and sugar. Mix well until all lumps are removed. Add flour. Mix well. Add nuts. Mix well. Spoon into 1 greased loaf pan. Bake at 350°F for 55-60 minutes.

Author's Comments

There is a little story behind the name of this bread... When I was working on the recipe, a friend of mine was pregnant. I brought her a loaf of banana bread every week for about 6-7 months until the baby was born. I was going to try like heck to help make this a healthy baby!

Devin was born on February 7th, 1998 a very healthy 9 lbs. 10 ounces! I was there at the hospital and got to see Christine hold him for the first time. What a wonderful experience...

Today Devin is celebrating his first birthday. It is with gratitude that I post this recipe; grateful am I to have shared in his birthing experience and grateful to see him today walking and playing.

Happy Birthday Devin! Glad you liked the bread! :) Ryan
