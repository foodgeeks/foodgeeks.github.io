Awesome Apple Bread

kathy_wetzel

Time

prep 0:15       total 1:55

Yield

2 loaves

Ingredients

Ingredients

1 cup oil
3 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
3 cups apples, diced
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions

Combine and set aside the oil, eggs, sugar, and vanilla. Sift flour, cinnamon, soda, and salt. Add dry ingredients to oil mixture gradually. Add apples and nuts. Bake in 2 regular loaf pans or 3 foil loaf pans for 1-1/2 hours at 300Â°F. Cool 10 minutes in the pan. Sprinkle with sugar, if desired.

Author's Comments

Note: I bake it at 325 degrees for about 70 minutes.
